I teach regularly at Morley College in London and West Dean College near Chichester; details of upcoming classes can be found at their websites by searching under 'papermaking'. Other recent teaching venues include the 2017 Summer School for the Association of Guilds of Weavers, Spinners and Dyers, East London Printmakers and the Barber Institute of Fine Arts at Birmingham University.

I also teach friendly, individualised papermaking workshops at Tidekettle Paper in Berwick-upon-Tweed, for  individuals and small groups of all ages; do send me a message via the contact page or the Facebook or Instagram link above to discuss your requirements. Options range from an Introduction to Papermaking for beginners to more specialised techniques, or even seasonal card-making workshops.

I have Qualified Teacher Status and welcome the opportunity to work with schools and other educational organisations. Details of past school workshops on request.

Papermaking Classes at Tidekettle

* An Introduction to Papermaking

2 students, 2½ hours, all materials and paper included, £35 per person for 2 people, £45 for one person. I can occasionally accommodate up to 4 people - just requires a bit more advance planning!

Please contact me to arrange a time that suits you - Sundays and Mondays are generally easiest as the shop is closed.

Other options include Garden Plant Papermaking, Japanese Fibres and Sheet forming, various 3D techniques, Paper for Artists' Books, Paste Papers and special projects of your own.

To book, please contact me on here, Facebook, Instagam, drop into the shop or call/text 07528 217004.

I teach regularly at Morley College in London and West Dean College near Chichester; details of upcoming classes can be found at their websites by searching under 'papermaking'. Other recent teaching venues include the 2017 Summer School for the Association of Guilds of Weavers, Spinners and Dyers, East London Printmakers and several London primary schools.

I also teach friendly, individualised papermaking workshops at Tidekettle Paper in Berwick-upon-Tweed, for groups of up to four people of all ages; do send me a message via the contact page or the Facebook link above to discuss your requirements. Options range from an Introduction to Papermaking for beginners to more specialised areas, or even holiday card-making workshops.

I have Qualified Teacher Status (in French and German) and welcome the opportunity to work with schools and other educational organisations.

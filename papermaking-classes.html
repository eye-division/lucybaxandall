I currently teach at West Dean College near Chichester; details of upcoming classes can be found at their website by searching under 'short courses' and 'papermaking'. I usually teach Creative Papermaking - Translucency and Light in January/February and Creative Papermaking - Layer, Emboss and Cast in June/July. If you are new to West Dean, take a look. The classes are like a retreat and a holiday as well as an intensive learning experience! 

I also participate in community events close to Berwick and publicise those on Instagram @lucypaperbax 

I'm not currently taking bookings for other workshops as I'm taking some time to concentrate on my own work - more details on the Tidekettle news page.
